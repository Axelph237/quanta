# About
## Motivation
My initial motivation behind Quanta was simply my **lack of surrounding community** as passionate about quantum computing as I am. This made me question the current state of quantum computing education as a whole. I mean, it seems to make sense. In my experience, most informational articles or textbooks are either **too vague** about the topic (many YouTube videos), or expect some **fundamental understanding** of quantum mechanics concepts that can make it hard for newly interested students to get started (such as the IBM textbook, though the blessing that it is). 

I wish that three years ago I had a **quick and easy** resource for dipping my toes into quantum computing. Here's to a wish answered 🥂. I hope you all enjoy it as much as I have come to love this field :)
